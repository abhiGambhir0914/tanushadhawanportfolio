The world of cryptocurrency runs on the dark web, still many countries with big economies are using. Coinmart provides a solution that helps the government to make cryptocurrency legal and profitable to the economy of our country as well.

Research & Analysis

User research focuses on understanding user behaviors, needs, and motivations through observation techniques, task analysis, and other feedback methodologies. For me user research is “the process of understanding the impact of design on an audience”.

Cryptocurrency

Bitcoin is the preeminent cryptocurrency and first to be used widely. However, hundreds of cryptocurrencies exist, and more spring into being every month. It uses cryptographic protocols or extremely complex code systems that encrypt sensitive data transfers, to secure their units of exchange. Also, they are protected by unbreakable cryptography which makes it a secure way to store wealth but also creates the risk that when a Bitcoin owners die, their digital fortune will be out of reach forever.


It uses the Private key for the transactions. A private key is like an unchangeable password, which is generated when you create a new cryptocurrency wallet. The key should always be kept as safe and secure as possible. Coin-base, the largest trading platform, puts the burden on the heirs to claim any assets left by the deceased.

Regulations

Until the technology is adopted by federal governments and regulated, there will be an increased risk of investing in this technology. The technology is mostly logistical. For example, changing protocols, which becomes necessary when the tech is being improved, can take quite a long time and interrupt the normal flow of operations.

Crypto-currency in India

The RBI has clamped down on crypto-currencies. As part of its bi-monthly monetary policy statement, the CBI stated that with immediate effect, entities regulated by its can no longer deal with or provide service to any individual or business or settling virtual currencies.


The exchange argues that an indiscriminate ban isn’t the way to deal with this. By accounts and allowing more cash related transactions RBI is allowing more people to get duped. The difficulty in tracking the source of money. The RBI has also expressed concern that cryptocurrency has no intrinsic value as they are not blocked by an asset.


It would not be possible due to its underlining technology which makes it easy for anyone to transfer or store. Cryptocurrency without any third party help.

Custodian Wallet Providers

Custodian wallet providers, on the contrary, have custody over the user’s public and private key, making them from a conceptual perspective quite similar to financial institutions holding bank or payment accounts. Therefore, they warrant more regulatory attention.
